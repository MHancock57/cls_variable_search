Descriptions of the available metadata

Metadata FieldMetadata Sub-fieldDescription
studyThe name of the cohort study that the variable is from
categoryThe category of data that the variable belongs to (e.g. geoggraphy, linked education data, linked health data, omics, substudy data, survey data)
research_topicThe broader concept(s) that are measured by this variable plus the specific concept(s) that are measured by this variable (note split by the ':' character)
sweep_year_ageThe sweep that the variable is from plus the year the sweep took place plus the age of the cohort member at the time of the sweep
variable_nameThe name of the variable in the depoisted dataset
variable_labelA short description of the variable
type_of_survey_dataType of survey data that the variable represents (e.g. survey responses, paradata, linked or biomeasure). Note populated only if the variable category is survey or substudy)
derivedWhether the variable represents derived data
UKDS_SNThe UKDS access level for the deposited dataset plus the study number that the dataset is included in at the UKDS
dataset_nameName of the UKDS deposited dataset that the variable is included in
